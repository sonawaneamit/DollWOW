# DollWow Launch Content Checklist

Use this as the review list before broad product publishing. The goal is to borrow category best practices without copying source-store language, names, or page structure.

## Product Page Education

- [ ] Write DollWow-original "what is included" copy for each major brand/material family.
- [ ] Add brand-specific option notes for paid add-ons, compatibility, and production-time implications.
- [ ] Add QC-photo expectations by supplier: available, sometimes available, or not promised.
- [ ] Add discreet delivery copy that explains packaging, billing, tracking, and support tone.
- [ ] Add care and storage guidance for TPE, silicone, hybrid, torso, and in-stock warehouse items.
- [ ] Add accessory explanations for care kits, stands, hanging brackets, cases, wigs, inserts, and storage.
- [ ] Add price-match explanation with approved-vendor, total-price, freshness, margin, and human-review rules.

## Product Data

- [ ] Confirm product title is rewritten for DollWow and not a Rosemary duplicate.
- [ ] Confirm product description is rewritten in DollWow voice and does not include source-store boilerplate.
- [ ] Confirm source URL/provenance is stored for internal review.
- [ ] Confirm product is not Rosemary-exclusive, celebrity/likeness-restricted, or authorization-limited.
- [ ] Confirm brand, material, height, weight, cup size, warehouse country, and delivery estimate.
- [ ] Confirm option groups are product-specific where needed, not assumed brand-wide.
- [ ] Confirm checkbox/add-on groups support multi-select and include a safe default such as "No add-on."
- [ ] Confirm paid options have imported prices and that expensive add-ons are visible before checkout.

## Imagery

- [ ] Confirm launch images are supplier-authorized, DollWow-owned, or approved demo assets.
- [ ] Keep product inspection images accurate and do not AI-alter actual product details.
- [ ] Label AI-generated editorial or option illustrations internally by provenance.
- [ ] Avoid underage-coded, school-themed, fake-buyer, fake-review, or deceptive imagery.
- [ ] Add missing option reference images where supplier provides them.
- [ ] Use generated illustrations only for generic option education when they do not misrepresent the final product.

## SEO

- [ ] Run DataForSEO enrichment on reviewed products before Shopify update when credentials are available.
- [ ] Keep SEO titles distinct from Rosemary/source titles while preserving factual specs.
- [ ] Write meta descriptions around buyer intent, material, size, brand, stock state, and customization value.
- [ ] Avoid copied product descriptions and repeated boilerplate across product pages.
- [ ] Add internal links to Help Me Choose, Warehouse, Compare, Shipping, Returns, and Support where relevant.
- [ ] Add structured FAQ content only when the answer is accurate for the supplier/product.

## Conversion And Trust

- [ ] Make price breakdown visible on every customizable product.
- [ ] Show checkout/review summary before sending selections to Shopify.
- [ ] Explain custom-order review, production timing, QC photo request, and shipment handoff.
- [ ] Add support CTA wording as "Ask our team" rather than awkward "human" labels.
- [ ] Add price-match CTA without promising automatic discounts when guardrails fail.
- [ ] Show verified reviews only after real DollWow orders exist.
