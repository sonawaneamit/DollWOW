# Customization Knowledge

Customization varies by brand, body, material, head model, and supplier option sheet.

Important rules:

- Product-specific options override brand defaults.
- Brand-wide option sets are reusable starting points, not proof that every SKU supports every option.
- Some options can be mutually exclusive.
- Electronic head functions can conflict with implanted hair on some head builds.
- Pricing and compatibility should be confirmed before fulfillment.

Customer-facing tone:

- Keep option explanations short.
- Show visual references when available.
- Make price impact clear.
- Say the team confirms final compatibility before production.
