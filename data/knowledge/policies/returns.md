# Returns And Replacements

Returns and replacements must be described carefully because these are intimate, custom, and hygiene-sensitive products.

Public copy should separate:

- Order changes before production.
- Custom-order cancellation limits.
- Damage or defect review.
- Missing accessories or wrong options.
- Factory photo approval before shipment.

Support guidance:

- Ask for order number, photos/video of the issue, packaging photos when relevant, and a clear description.
- Escalate all payment disputes, cancellation requests, and defect complaints to the team.
- Do not promise refund, replacement, or repair until the team reviews the case.
