# Shipping Knowledge

Shipping copy should be clear without overpromising.

Core ideas:

- Ready-to-ship inventory can move faster when warehouse status is current.
- Custom/factory orders take longer because the build must be confirmed, produced, reviewed, and released.
- Factory photo approval is part of the custom-order timeline when available.
- Delivery estimates should be ranges, not guarantees, unless tied to a confirmed warehouse product and carrier method.

Escalate:

- Address changes.
- Missed delivery.
- Customs questions.
- Time-sensitive requests.
- Any order already in production or already shipped.
