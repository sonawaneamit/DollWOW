# Discreet Delivery And Privacy

DollWow should make privacy feel calm and practical.

Approved public themes:

- Plain outer packaging where available.
- Neutral billing/checkout language where supported by Shopify/payment settings.
- Private support communication.
- No product-identifying language on public tracking or delivery copy where avoidable.

Avoid:

- Overpromising that no carrier, customs office, or payment provider can infer anything.
- Jokes or euphemisms that make the buyer feel exposed.
- Fake claims about packaging that have not been confirmed for a supplier or warehouse path.
