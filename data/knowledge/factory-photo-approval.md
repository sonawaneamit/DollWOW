# Factory Photo Approval

Factory photo approval is a core DollWow trust promise.

Plain-language explanation:

- For custom orders, the supplier sends factory photos and, when available, videos before shipment.
- DollWow shares those with the customer for approval.
- The customer can approve the build or ask for revisions before release.
- This reduces surprises on delivery and makes the long production window feel visible.

Important wording:

- Use "factory photos" or "factory photo approval."
- Do not imply every supplier provides the same number of photos or videos.
- Do not promise unlimited revisions unless the supplier/order situation supports it.
- Do not publish private order photos without approval for DollWow proof-gallery use.
