# Price Match Knowledge

DollWow can review competitor listings from trusted retailers and authorized vendors.

Customer-facing principles:

- We compare the full delivered value, not just the headline price.
- We look at product match, configuration, stock status, shipping, coupon codes, and visible promotions.
- If a deal needs review, the customer should know our team is checking it rather than receiving a vague failure.

Automation principles:

- Unknown vendors do not receive automatic price matching.
- Approved vendors and trusted Doll Forum vendors can be eligible for faster review.
- Live or very fresh crawl data is required for any automatic discount.
- Ambiguous promotions, unusually aggressive discounts, unclear freebies, or uncertain product matches must create a team-review lead.
- Supplier cost, margin floor, MAP constraints, and discount caps must be enforced by code before any automatic code is issued.
