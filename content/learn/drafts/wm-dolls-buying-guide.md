## Common Mistakes

The biggest mistake is treating "WM Dolls" as one product type. A brand can have many bodies, heads, sizes, and option paths. Another mistake is choosing by photo without checking weight, storage needs, and whether the shown configuration matches what is included.

If a detail affects the order, confirm it before checkout.

## Buyer Checklist

Before choosing a WM doll, confirm:

- Exact body and head
- Material and mixed construction if relevant
- Height, weight, and measurements
- Ready-to-ship or custom order path
- Included wig, eyes, accessories, or care items
- Available skin tone, hair, feet, skeleton, and function options
- Production timing if custom
- Whether current photos show the selected configuration

This checklist keeps the decision grounded in the product and the brand search.
