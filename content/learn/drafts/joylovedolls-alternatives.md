## Buyer Checklist

- Are you comparing the same category on both sites?
- Does the page show exact product details or only broad marketing copy?
- Are material and measurements clear?
- Does the listing explain ready-to-ship, check-stock, or custom order status?
- Are custom options specific to the product?
- Does the seller explain discreet delivery expectations?
- Can support confirm the details before payment?
- Is the total delivered value still clear after options and shipping?

## Common Mistakes

### Comparing broad catalogs too early

Broad store pages help discovery, but they do not answer whether a specific doll is the right fit.

### Ignoring body type and measurements

For male dolls especially, body scale and weight can change handling, storage, and fit more than styling alone.

### Assuming best-selling means best for you

Popularity can point to common buyer preferences. It cannot replace a fit check.
