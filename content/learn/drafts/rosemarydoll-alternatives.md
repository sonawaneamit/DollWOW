## Buyer Checklist

- Is the product name specific and free from obvious mismatch?
- Do the listed photos match the material and body type?
- Are height, weight, and measurements visible?
- Is the doll ready to ship, custom order, or check-stock?
- Are custom options confirmed for that exact product?
- Does the page explain discreet delivery expectations?
- Can support confirm the order details before payment?
- Does the final delivered price still make sense after shipping and options?

## Common Mistakes

### Copying specs from one site to another

Even when products look similar, details can change by listing, supplier, stock path, and option set. Confirm the facts on the page you are actually using.

### Treating TPE as one quality level

TPE is a material category, not a single product quality. Size, care, finish, and supplier formulation matter.

### Assuming custom options are brand-wide

Some options vary by body, head, material, or production rules. Ask before checkout if an option matters.
