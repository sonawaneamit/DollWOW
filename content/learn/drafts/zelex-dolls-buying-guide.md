## Common Mistakes

Do not let premium presentation replace product checks. A beautiful image does not answer weight, stock path, option compatibility, or included-item questions. Also confirm whether a product photo shows the exact selected configuration.

## Buyer Checklist

Before choosing a Zelex doll, confirm:

- Exact material and head/body construction
- Height, weight, and measurements
- Whether the shown hair and eyes are included
- Whether the listing is ready-to-ship or custom
- Which options are supported for the model
- Whether production timing changes with customization
- Storage and handling expectations

If realism is your priority, compare the whole build rather than one face photo.
