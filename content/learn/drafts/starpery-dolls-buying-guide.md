# Starpery Dolls Buying Guide

By Alex, Doll Collector and DollWow Product Educator with 20+ Years of Experience

## Quick Answer

Starpery Dolls are often evaluated for realistic styling, silicone-focused builds, and strong product-photo presentation. Buyers should compare the exact model by material, height, weight, face and body pairing, finish, customization path, and stock or production timing. Do not rely on images alone when the final configuration matters.

## Key Takeaways

- Starpery comparison often starts with realism and finish.
- Silicone-focused listings still need weight and storage checks.
- Product images should be matched to exact configuration.
- Custom options should be confirmed for the specific product.
- DollWow support can help verify unclear details before checkout.

## How To Compare Starpery Dolls

Use the [Starpery Dolls brand hub](/brands/starpery-dolls) as the commercial starting point. From there, compare material, size, body proportions, listed measurements, and whether the product is ready-to-ship or factory order.

## Common Mistakes

The common mistake is choosing by face photo alone. A realistic face on the wrong body size, material, or weight may not fit the buyer's needs. Another mistake is assuming the image shows the default configuration.

## Buyer Checklist

Before choosing a Starpery doll, confirm:

- Exact head, body, and material
- Height, weight, and full measurements
- Whether shown hair, eyes, outfit, or accessories are included
- Ready-to-ship or factory-order status
- Which custom options are supported for the model
- Whether production timing changes with customization
- Cleaning, storage, and handling expectations

If realism is the priority, compare the whole build rather than one polished face photo.
