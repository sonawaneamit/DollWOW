## Buyer Checklist

Before choosing an SE Doll listing, confirm:

- Exact material and head/body pairing
- Height, weight, and measurements
- Included items versus optional accessories
- Ready-to-ship or factory-order status
- Supported custom options for the exact model
- Whether the displayed photos match the selected configuration
- Support confirmation for unclear product details

This makes the brand comparison useful without relying on broad assumptions.
