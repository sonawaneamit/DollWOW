## Common Mistakes

Do not assume a dramatic product photo tells the whole order story. Styling, costume pieces, hair, and accessories may not be included. Also avoid choosing a larger build without checking handling weight and storage.

## Buyer Checklist

Before choosing an Irontech doll, confirm:

- Exact body type and head pairing
- Material and head/body construction
- Height, weight, shoulder width, and storage needs
- Included styling versus optional costume or accessory items
- Skeleton type, standing feet, and posing support
- Ready-to-ship or factory-order path
- Which custom options are supported for the model

This keeps the comparison practical, especially for larger builds, male dolls, and heavily styled product photos.
