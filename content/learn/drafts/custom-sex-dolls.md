# Custom Sex Dolls: What You Can Usually Change

By Alex, Doll Collector and DollWow Product Educator with 20+ Years of Experience

## Quick Answer

A custom sex doll usually starts with a base model, then allows selected changes such as skin tone, eyes, wig or hair, makeup style, feet, skeleton, heating, and other supplier-supported options. The important word is usually. Custom availability depends on the brand, body, head, material, current supplier rules, and production path, so every meaningful option should be confirmed before checkout.

## Key Takeaways

- Customization starts with the exact base model.
- Options can vary by brand, body, head, height, material, and supplier.
- Some options are mutually exclusive or require extra production time.
- Final price can change with upgrades, shipping, and supplier confirmation.
- DollWow support can help confirm the current option path before payment.

## Start With The Base Model

Custom ordering works best when the buyer chooses the right base model first. The body, head, material, height, weight, and measurements set the practical limits for everything else.

Do not assume a customization menu applies to every product from a brand. A supplier may support an option on one body and not another. A head function may affect hair choices. A standing-foot option may not be recommended for every body type. The base product page should be the starting point.

## Compatibility Matters

The most expensive custom mistake is assuming that every appealing option can be combined. Some options may conflict. For example, certain electronic head functions may not pair well with implanted hair on some brands because wiring, scalp construction, and hair installation can affect the same area.

Other compatibility checks can involve material, skeleton, feet, body size, or supplier rules. This is why DollWow treats customization as product-specific. Brand-wide defaults are useful starting points, but the exact SKU should win.

## Ready-To-Ship vs Custom

Ready-to-ship listings are usually more fixed. A warehouse-style product may be available faster, but the configuration is often already set. Custom orders give more control, but they can require supplier confirmation, production time, approval materials, and final shipping checks.

## What To Ask Before Checkout

Ask:

- Which options are available for this exact product?
- Which choices affect price?
- Which choices affect production time?
- Are any options incompatible?
- Are reference images available for the option?
- What is included in the base price?
- What needs supplier confirmation?

If an answer affects your decision, get it confirmed before payment.

## FAQs

### Can I customize every sex doll?

No. Customization depends on the exact brand, body, head, material, and supplier rules. Some listings are more flexible than others.

### What custom options are most common?

Common options include skin tone, eyes, hair, makeup, feet, skeleton upgrades, heating, and selected accessories. Availability varies by product.

### Do custom options change the price?

They can. Some options are included, some are paid upgrades, and some may affect shipping or production timing.

### Are ready-to-ship dolls customizable?

Often less so. Ready-to-ship listings usually have a fixed configuration, although support can confirm what can still be changed.
